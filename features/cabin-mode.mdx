Cabin Mode allows the pilot or co-pilot of the UH-1H and Blackhawk to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely **inside the helicopter's cabin** rather than exiting to the outside. Full WASD movement, weapon use, and aiming down sights (ADS) are available while inside.

## Supported Helicopters

| Helicopter | Cabin Mode |
|------------|:----------:|
| MD500 Civilian | ✗ |
| MH-6 Little Bird | ✗ |
| SA342M Gazelle | ✗ |
| **UH-1H Iroquois** | ✓ |
| **MH-60L Blackhawk** | ✓ |
| AW101 Merlin | ✗ |

## How to Use

### Entering the Cabin

1. Be seated as **pilot or co-pilot**
2. Look back to find the **"Move to Cabin"** action
3. Activate it — your character leaves their cockpit seat and walks freely inside the helicopter's cabin, moving with the aircraft in flight

### Inside the Cabin

- Full **WASD movement** — walk around inside the helicopter
- **Weapon use** — draw and fire weapons
- **ADS** — aim down sights normally
- Physical **wall boundaries** prevent walking through the fuselage

### Returning to the Cockpit

At any time, use the **"Return to Cockpit"** action to return to your seat. You are placed back in your original pilot or co-pilot seat if it is free; otherwise in the alternate cockpit seat.

### What Happens to the Helicopter

When the pilot enters Cabin Mode, they leave the controls. The helicopter does **not** automatically hold position — what happens next depends on who else is aboard:

- **Co-pilot present** — the co-pilot retains full flight controls and can continue flying normally
- **No co-pilot** — the helicopter is uncrewed and will drift freely on whatever heading and collective it had when the pilot left

<Tip>
  If you plan to enter Cabin Mode solo, engage **Auto Hover** (`X`) first. This locks the helicopter into a stable hold before you leave the seat, keeping it stationary while you are in the cabin.
</Tip>

## Server Configuration

| Setting | What it does | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| `CZH_cabinModeEnabled` | `1` = Cabin Mode enabled. `0` = disabled server-wide for all helicopters. | `1` |

<Note>
  Cabin Mode is a cockpit crew feature only. Cabin passengers use the standard get-out action which exits them into the interior — they do not need or have the "Move to Cabin" action.
</Note>

## Native Cabin Linking

Separate from Cabin Mode, the UH-1H, MH-60L Blackhawk, AW101 Merlin, and MH-6 all support **native DayZ cabin linking**. When a player physically walks into or stands on the interior/rear opening of these helicopters, they are automatically linked to the aircraft and travel with it — no action required. This applies even mid-flight.

| Helicopter | How it works |
|------------|-------------|
| **AW101 Merlin** | All seats exit into the enclosed fuselage interior — players are linked natively on seat exit and when standing inside the aircraft. Rear ramp must be open to board or disembark. |
| **UH-1H Iroquois** | Players can walk onto the rear cabin opening and link natively, in addition to using Cabin Mode from the cockpit. |
| **MH-60L Blackhawk** | Same as UH-1H — rear opening supports native linking alongside Cabin Mode. |
| **MH-6 Little Bird** | The small rear platform area supports native linking — stepping onto it links the player to the aircraft. |

<Note>
  Native cabin linking is handled by the DayZ engine directly. It is not configurable through the mod's JSON settings.
</Note>
