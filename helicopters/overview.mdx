## Cabin Mode & Free Walk

Three systems allow players to move freely inside a moving helicopter:

**Cabin Mode** (UH-1H, MH-60L Blackhawk) — the pilot or co-pilot exits their cockpit seat outward by default. Cabin Mode provides an alternative: use the **Move to Cabin** action to leave the cockpit and walk freely inside the cabin with full WASD, weapon use, and ADS. **Return to Cockpit** puts them straight back. Server owners can disable it globally via `CZH_cabinModeEnabled = 0` in `mod_config.json`.

**Seat Exit Free Walk** (UH-1H, MH-60L, AW101 Merlin) — cabin passenger seats on the UH-1H and Blackhawk exit into the enclosed interior via the standard DayZ "Get Out" action; players link to the aircraft automatically. On the AW101 Merlin this applies to **all seats including pilot and co-pilot** — the entire fuselage is enclosed, so Get Out always places the player inside. The rear ramp must be open to actually disembark.

**Walk-On Linking** (UH-1H, MH-60L, MH-6 Little Bird, AW101 Merlin) — stepping onto the rear cabin opening, the MH-6's external side bench platform, or the Merlin's rear ramp area automatically links a player to the aircraft. They travel with it until they step off. No action required.

See [Cabin Mode](/features/cabin-mode) for full details on all three systems.
