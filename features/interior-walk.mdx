Interior Walk (also referred to as **Cabin Mode**) allows the pilot or co-pilot of supported helicopters to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely **inside the helicopter's cabin** rather than exiting to the outside. Full WASD movement, weapon use, and aiming down sights (ADS) are available while inside.

<Note>
  On helicopters **without** Cabin Mode, exiting the cockpit seat puts the player outside the aircraft through the door. Cabin Mode is exclusive to the UH-1H Iroquois and MH-60L Blackhawk.
</Note>

## Supported Helicopters

| Helicopter | Cabin Mode |
|------------|:----------:|
| MD500 Civilian | ✗ |
| MH-6 Little Bird | ✗ |
| SA342M Gazelle | ✗ |
| **UH-1H Iroquois** | ✓ |
| **MH-60L Blackhawk** | ✓ |
| AW101 Merlin | ✗ |

## How to Use

### Entering the Cabin

1. Be seated as **pilot or co-pilot**
2. Look back to find the **"Move to Cabin"** action
3. Activate it — your character leaves their cockpit seat and walks freely inside the helicopter's cabin, moving with the aircraft in flight

### Inside the Cabin

- Full **WASD movement** — walk around inside the helicopter
- **Weapon use** — draw and fire weapons
- **ADS** — aim down sights normally
- Physical **wall boundaries** prevent walking through the fuselage

### Returning to the Cockpit

At any time, use the **"Return to Cockpit"** action to return to your seat. You are placed back in your original pilot or co-pilot seat if it is free; otherwise in the alternate cockpit seat.

## Server Configuration

| Setting | What it does | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| `CZH_cabinModeEnabled` | `1` = Cabin Mode enabled. `0` = disabled server-wide for all helicopters. | `1` |

<Note>
  Cabin Mode is a cockpit crew feature only. Standard cabin passengers cannot enter walk mode — only the pilot and co-pilot have the "Move to Cabin" action.
</Note>

---

## AW101 Merlin — Native Cabin Behaviour

The Merlin does **not** use Cabin Mode. However, it behaves differently to the smaller helicopters — because the Merlin is a large fully enclosed fuselage, **exiting any seat places the player inside the aircraft**, not outside. Players are automatically linked to the helicopter when inside the fuselage and travel with it — including mid-flight. The rear ramp must be open for players to board or disembark.
