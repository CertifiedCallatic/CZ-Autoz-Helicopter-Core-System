# AW101 Merlin

The AW101 Merlin is the largest helicopter in the mod, capable of carrying 18 people. With its rear loading ramp, retractable landing gear, and enclosed fuselage, it is purpose-built for large group transport. All passengers exit inside the fuselage, making the rear ramp essential for efficient boarding and disembarkation.

## Rear Ramp

The Merlin has an openable rear loading ramp:

- Use the **ramp action** while grounded to open or close it
- Practical for large group boarding — all passengers enter and exit through the rear
- Ramp action is available to pilot and co-pilot

## Retractable Landing Gear

The Merlin's landing gear retracts and extends automatically — no manual input is required from the pilot. Gear state is managed by the flight system.
