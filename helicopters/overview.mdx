## Interior Walk

Interior Walk allows the pilot or co-pilot to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely inside the cabin while in flight, with full WASD movement, weapon use, and ADS.

Available on:
- **UH-1H Iroquois** — all variants
- **MH-60L Blackhawk** — all variants

Server owners can disable it globally via `CZH_cabinModeEnabled = 0` in `mod_config.json`.

See [Interior Walk](/features/interior-walk) for full details.
