Interior Walk allows crew to leave their cockpit seat and move freely inside the helicopter while airborne. Full WASD movement, weapon use, and aiming down sights (ADS) are available. The system works differently depending on which helicopter you are on.

## Supported Helicopters

| Helicopter | Interior Walk | System |
|------------|:------------:|--------|
| MD500 Civilian | ✗ | — |
| MH-6 Little Bird | ✗ | — |
| SA342M Gazelle | ✗ | — |
| **UH-1H Iroquois** | ✓ | Cockpit exit → cabin walk |
| **MH-60L Blackhawk** | ✓ | Cockpit exit → cabin walk |
| **AW101 Merlin** | ✓ | Open-cabin link system |

---

## UH-1H & Blackhawk — Cockpit Exit Walk

### Entering the Cabin

1. Be seated as **pilot or co-pilot**
2. Look for the **"Move to Cabin"** interaction action
3. Activate it — after a short 1.5-second transition your character is linked to the helicopter's coordinate space and moves with it in flight

### Inside the Cabin

- Full **WASD movement** — walk around inside the helicopter
- **Weapon use** — draw and fire weapons
- **ADS** — aim down sights normally
- Physical **wall boundaries** prevent walking through the fuselage

### Returning to the Cockpit

1. Look for the **"Return to Cockpit"** action
2. Activate it — your character re-seats in the cockpit
3. Returns to your original seat (pilot or co-pilot) if free; otherwise places you in the alternate cockpit seat

<Note>
  On the UH-1H and Blackhawk, Interior Walk is a cockpit crew feature only. Standard cabin passengers cannot enter walk mode — only the pilot and co-pilot have the "Move to Cabin" action.
</Note>

## AW101 Merlin — Open-Cabin Link System

The Merlin uses a different, more open approach. The cargo cabin is fully enclosed and large enough to move around freely. Players are **linked to the aircraft's coordinate space** whenever they are inside, so they travel with the helicopter in flight regardless of whether they are seated or on foot.

**How it works:**

- **Walk up the ramp** and enter the fuselage — you are automatically linked to the helicopter and will travel with it
- **Exit a seat** while inside — you remain linked and can walk freely inside the cabin
- **Take a seat** from inside — you are seated normally, still linked
- **Walk off the ramp** or exit the aircraft — you are unlinked and return to normal on-foot movement

This means the Merlin can be boarded on the ground with the ramp open, passengers can move between seats or stand in the cabin in flight, and disembarking is as simple as walking out.
