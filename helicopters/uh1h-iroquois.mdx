The iconic Huey. The UH-1H is a 5-seat medium helicopter and one of two aircraft in the mod with Interior Walk — the ability for the pilot or co-pilot to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely inside the cabin while the helicopter is in flight.

<Note>
  There is no plain `UH1H` classname — all four variants are named. Use one of the classnames from the table below.
</Note>

## Interior Walk

The UH-1H supports the Interior Walk system:

1. While seated as **pilot or co-pilot**, look back to find the **"Move to Cabin"** action
2. Activate it — your character leaves their cockpit seat and walks freely inside the cabin, moving with the helicopter during flight
3. Full **WASD movement**, **weapon use**, and **ADS** are available inside the cabin
4. At any time, use **"Return to Cockpit"** to return to your seat (original seat if free, otherwise alternate)

<Warning>
  Interior Walk can be disabled server-wide by setting `CZH_cabinModeEnabled = 0` in `mod_config.json`.
</Warning>

See [Interior Walk](/features/interior-walk) for full details.
