# UH-1H Iroquois

The iconic Huey. The UH-1H is a 5-seat medium helicopter and one of two aircraft in the mod with Interior Walk — the ability for the pilot or co-pilot to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely inside the cabin while the helicopter is in flight.

## Specifications

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Total Seats** | 5 |
| **Cockpit** | 2 (pilot + co-pilot) |
| **Cabin** | 3 |
| **Interior Walk** | ✓ Yes |
| **Startup Time** | ~49 seconds |
| **Config File** | `UH1H_config.json` |

## Interior Walk

The UH-1H supports the Interior Walk system:

1. While seated as **pilot or co-pilot**, look for the **"Move to Cabin"** action
2. The player exits their seat and is linked to the helicopter's coordinate space — they move with the helicopter during flight
3. Full **WASD movement**, **weapon use**, and **ADS** are available inside the cabin
4. Use the **"Return to Cockpit"** action to re-seat in the cockpit (returns to original seat if free, otherwise the alternate seat)

<Warning>
  Interior Walk can be disabled server-wide by setting `CZH_cabinModeEnabled = 0` in `mod_config.json`.
</Warning>

See [Interior Walk](/features/interior-walk) for full technical details.
