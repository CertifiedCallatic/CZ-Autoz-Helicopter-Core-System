Interior Walk (also referred to as **Cabin Mode**) allows the pilot or co-pilot of supported helicopters to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely **inside the helicopter's cabin** rather than exiting to the outside. Full WASD movement, weapon use, and aiming down sights (ADS) are available while inside.

<Note>
  On helicopters **without** Cabin Mode, exiting the cockpit seat puts the player outside the aircraft through the door — there is no interior to walk into. Cabin Mode is a feature exclusive to the UH-1H Iroquois and MH-60L Blackhawk.
</Note>

## Supported Helicopters

| Helicopter | Cabin Mode |
|------------|:----------:|
| MD500 Civilian | ✗ |
| MH-6 Little Bird | ✗ |
| SA342M Gazelle | ✗ |
| **UH-1H Iroquois** | ✓ |
| **MH-60L Blackhawk** | ✓ |
| AW101 Merlin | ✗ |

## How to Use

### Entering the Cabin

1. Be seated as **pilot or co-pilot**
2. Look for the **"Move to Cabin"** interaction action
3. Activate it — after a short transition your character exits the cockpit seat and enters the helicopter's interior cabin, linked to the aircraft's coordinate space so you move with it in flight

### Inside the Cabin

- Full **WASD movement** — walk around inside the helicopter
- **Weapon use** — draw and fire weapons
- **ADS** — aim down sights normally
- Physical **wall boundaries** prevent walking through the fuselage

### Returning to the Cockpit

1. Look for the **"Return to Cockpit"** action
2. Activate it — your character re-seats in the cockpit
3. Returns to your original seat (pilot or co-pilot) if free; otherwise places you in the alternate cockpit seat

## Server Configuration

| Setting | What it does | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| `CZH_cabinModeEnabled` | `1` = Cabin Mode enabled. `0` = disabled server-wide for all helicopters. | `1` |

<Note>
  Cabin Mode is a cockpit crew feature only. Standard cabin passengers cannot enter walk mode — only the pilot and co-pilot have the "Move to Cabin" action.
</Note>
