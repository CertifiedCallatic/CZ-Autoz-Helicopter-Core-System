# Helicopter Overview

CZ Autoz Helicopter includes six distinct aircraft, ranging from the nimble 4-seat MD500 to the 18-seat AW101 Merlin. Each has its own flight characteristics, damage model, and set of variants.

## Quick Reference

| Helicopter | Seats | Interior Walk | Special Features | Startup |
|------------|-------|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| [MD500 Civilian](/helicopters/md500-civilian) | 4 (2 cockpit + 2 rear, open-air) | — | 4 variants, removable doors | ~17 s |
| [MH-6 Little Bird](/helicopters/mh6-little-bird) | 6 (2 cockpit + 4 side benches) | — | Grab-hold auto-attach | ~17 s |
| [SA342M Gazelle](/helicopters/sa342m-gazelle) | 4 (2 cockpit + 2 cabin) | — | Reversed cockpit layout | ~32 s |
| [UH-1H Iroquois](/helicopters/uh1h-iroquois) | 5 (2 cockpit + 3 cabin) | ✓ | 4 variants | ~49 s |
| [MH-60L Blackhawk](/helicopters/mh60l-blackhawk) | 8 (2 cockpit + 6 cabin) | ✓ | Sliding windows, 2 variants | ~20 s |
| [AW101 Merlin](/helicopters/aw101-merlin) | 18 (2 cockpit + 16 interior) | — | Rear ramp, retractable gear | ~60 s |

## Interior Walk

Interior Walk allows the pilot or co-pilot to leave their cockpit seat and walk freely inside the cabin while the helicopter is in flight, with full WASD movement, weapon use, and ADS.

This feature is available on:
- **UH-1H Iroquois** — all variants
- **MH-60L Blackhawk** — all variants

Server owners can disable it globally via `CZH_cabinModeEnabled = 0` in `mod_config.json`.

See [Interior Walk](/features/interior-walk) for full details.
