# AW101 Merlin

The AW101 Merlin is the largest and heaviest helicopter in the mod, capable of carrying 18 people. With its rear loading ramp, retractable tricycle landing gear, and large enclosed fuselage, it is purpose-built for large group transport. All passengers exit inside the fuselage — the rear ramp is essential for efficient boarding.

## Rear Ramp

The Merlin has an openable rear loading ramp:

- Use the **ramp action** while grounded to open or close it
- All 16 passengers enter and exit through the rear of the fuselage
- Practical for large group boarding — open the ramp, load up, close and lift off
- Ramp action available to pilot and co-pilot

## Retractable Landing Gear

The Merlin uses a tricycle landing gear setup that retracts and extends automatically — no manual input is required from the pilot. Gear is managed by the flight system.
